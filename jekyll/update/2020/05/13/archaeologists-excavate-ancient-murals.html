Arcaheologists Excavate Ancient Murals

May 13, 2020 | 03:10

Mister Mjir

News

Recently a famous archaeologist group (of whose name is unknown) recently excavated ancient murals from 5000 B.C. The team was very careful in ensuring they did not damage any of the murals, as they were highly fragile.

“Ya know it’s oua job to dig up these fossils and things, its pretty cool. Get ya hands dirty and find cool rocks.”

-Gilderoy Bones

Archaeologists are able to teach us about the culture and history of our world. Their work is invaluable to history teachers as it gives them more homework to give to their students. These murals express the human nature and are rooted in deep symbolism. The archaeologists have also taken the liberties to colorize the pictures to make them even more realistsic.
